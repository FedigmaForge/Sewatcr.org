Our Journey

Founded in 2013 by Samarjeet Tripathi, SEWA Charitable Trust began its humble journey in the vibrant communities of Mumbai's Dharavi and Prayagraj. What started as an informal initiative during Samarjeet's Economics graduation and CA/CS studies soon grew into a passionate movement dedicated to social upliftment.

In 2017, SEWA made Delhi its headquarters, steadily expanding with branches in Kanpur, Chitrakoot, Mahoba, Mumbai, and Lalpur (Chhattisgarh) ‚Äî broadening its footprint of impact across India.

Our TCR Philosophy - Transformation, Compassion, and Resilience guides every initiative we undertake, ensuring sustainable change that empowers communities to become self-reliant.
